Get help

Are you blocked with a bug you’re trying to fix, or a new feature you’re working on? Do you want to report harassment or a code of conduct violation? You’ve come to the right place!

Have questions about using Hoodie?

We’re in the process of making our documentation even better, so if you have a question related to using Hoodie, join our public Slack community and ask for help in the #help channel!


When nothing, oh nothing is going right.

Do you want to contribute to Hoodie?

Whether you’re a developer or a non-coding expert, we have a lot for everyone to do. If you’re already actively contributing — or are working on your first contribution — here are some resources to help you along the way:

  • Discover the ways you can contribute to Hoodie here.
  • If you’re running into blockers with your contribution, join our public Slack community and jump into the following channels:
    • #dev if you have technical questions
    • #editorial if you have writing/editing questions
    • #help if you aren’t sure where to direct a question to (the #help channel is indeed helpful for any kind of question!)
    • Or, if you want to get in touch with us through different means, there are other ways to contact us!!

Do you want to report a code of conduct violation?

We are here to help! If you’re being harassed, noticed someone else being harassed, or have any other concerns, please contact us immediately (reach out to Jan Lehnhardt on Twitter or email). Your reports will be taken seriously and will not be dismissed or argued with. And if you haven’t already done so, please read through our Code of Conduct!

We want Hoodie to have a bright and shiny future, and we'd be happy if you work with us on it.
