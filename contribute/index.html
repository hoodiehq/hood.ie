Contribute

Hoodie wouldn’t be where it is today without the people who supported it by dedicating their time, skills, knowledge, by giving talks about it and supporting the project financially.

Here at Hoodie’s, there’s always a lot of work to do for everyone, for example for people who do programming, design, writing, organising and many more. We ❤ the people who are involved in this project, and we’d love to have you on board.

If you want to take a first look at what we've been working recently, a good start may be to look at our Milestones, the Ubersicht of our activity on Hoodie's GitHub activity, — and of course our Blog and our Twitter always offer the latest information as well.

So here's to you, lovely person who wants to join us — this is how you can support us:

Share your skills and knowledge:

Hoodie is still work in progress, and you can help us make it more awesome. Support us in working towards Hoodie’s eventual goals and making Hoodie a tool which enables people to express themselves through a well-accessible, easy-to-learn technology. No matter your level of skills or knowledge, we appreciate your support and would love to welcome you on board.


Not sure if you want to join us? This is what we're like (almost).

Frontend Development

We need you, frontend development people!

  • Build your own app based on Hoodie,
  • help us with our currently open frontend development tasks,
  • support us with our integration of PouchDB into Hoodie by working on these issues, or
  • help us with the cleanup and fixing after our hoodie.js refactoring – all open issues for this topic are here.

If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to get in touch.


Backend Development

In order to enable many people so they can finally build apps without having to think about backends, Hoodie of course needs a backend itself. If you want to help with backend development, take a look at these currently open issues, help us with our building plugins, check our open backend tasks in our Milestones App or contact us!

Build Plugins to extend Hoodie’s core functionalities

Hoodie’s main features like user signup and administration, data storage and shares are all Node.js-based plugins which are added to its core code. All currently available Hoodie-Plugins are here. We encourage you to write your own plugins and publish them on npm so everyone can use them. In this tutorial you’ll see how building plugins works. If you need any help, let us know.


Design

We love great design, good User Interfaces and seamless User Experiences, as much as we love beautiful logos and illustrations, and we care a lot about all of them. Our currently open design tasks are e.g. creating graphics and designs for our Hoodie-Shop, helping with our graphical interfaces, making improvements on our content and of course anything you'd like to help us with (you're the expert here and your feedback would help us a lot). If you’re interested in helping us, please contact us, it would be lovely to hear from you.


Write content

We want to provide good content that is useful to people and that helps them understand what Hoodie is, does, stands for and what it can do for them. This is why we have a Blog which we frequently add new pieces to, and we have our ”TGIF”, the reading recommendations which we curate and publish every week. Oh, and we send out newsletters and do Social Media things and there's so much more we'd love to do. If you want to support our content people, contact us, it would be so great to have you on board!


Write documentation

Good projects are nothing without good documentation. And documentation is very important for us who want to empower people. If you want to help us by writing documentation yourself, proofreading and testing existing docs, translating content or creating screencasts: there you go!


Give feedback about Hoodie and its community

Did you try Hoodie, build something based on it or did you have a nice encounter with the Hoodie community? We want to improve in everything we do, and thus would love to hear your feedback. And here’s a tiny form for that (and you can of course also contact us).



Give talks

Do you like Hoodie and now want to tell other people about it? Wow, that’s amazing to hear! We know that preparing talks takes a lot of time. To support you and lower your efforts, we have prepared some talks about Hoodie for you, including presenter notes. Just click on the format of your choice, download them, adjust them to your needs and help us spread the word! Our currently available talks are (zip archive including fonts):

  • "One million Cheesecakes — Getting started with Hoodie": Keynote
  • "Coding the Dream: Getting started with Hoodie and Offline First": Keynote

If you're interested in other talks or topics, you'll find all our past talks, usually with slides, in this list. If you then would like to present one of them yourself, please contact us.


Start a Meetup in your town

Hoodie is both easy-to-learn for beginners and a great tool for experienced developers. So why not start a Hoodie Meetup in your town? Many of us have been organising meetups for a long time now and found these three pieces really helpful:

  • How to organise your own meetup by Kate Kendall
  • A practical guide to organising a successful Meetup by Jordan Lewis
  • 25 Best Practices for Meetup Organizers by Mike Schinkel

If you want to give a Hoodie-talk at your meetup, check the “give talks”-section above, we have talks ready for you to adjust and use.

Or maybe you want to host a Hoodie Workshop? Then we got something for you … We have prepared a document with prerequisites and a running order here, it's still work in progress but also has links to tutorials or content you may want to use and some useful tips. For your workshop participants, there's also a Hoodie Cheat Sheet, you can get it here: Web Version | Print Version.

In case you need further support, ping us, we’re happy to help!


Wear Hoodie

Cozy, warm and snuggly: this is Hoodie. And this is what Hoodie textiles are like. You’ll find them all in our Hoodie-Merchandise-Shop. We have some default items there, and some special offers which are only available for a limited time. By getting your own Hoodie-Shirt, Hoodie-Tote or whatever you may like, you also support us financially.


Give money

Hoodie matters to us and we want it to have a good future and be a sustainable project. We also want to rethink the ways Open Source is built and funded, and we want to work towards a cultural change. Thus, we’re trying out different ways to fund it. And you can support us:

Sponsor Hoodie

We offer a sponsoring package for companies who are hiring new employees (or just want to sponsor us). Find more details on our sponsoring page.

Send us a Gratipay

Do you want to send us a small sum on a regular basis? We have a Gratipay account and are grateful for donations there.


Hire us!

Do you want us to build a prototype, an application or do you need consulting? There’s The Neighbourhoodie which offers these services. The company is dedicated to furthering Hoodie, and by hiring them, you help us pay people to work on the Open Source project and contribute to Hoodie.
